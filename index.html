Mission

FSFV stands beside veterans and their families with direct services and trusted referrals. We focus on stability first — safe housing, income pathways, and access to earned benefits — while promoting long‑term independence and community belonging.

As a 501(c)(3) organization, We operate with transparency and compliance, contributions may be tax‑deductible to the extent allowed by law. EIN: 85-1436299.

Housing & Utilities

Short‑term assistance, deposit/utility aid, and referrals to stable housing programs.

Benefits & Navigation

Guidance on VA/DoD benefits, healthcare enrollment, and essential documentation.

Employment & Wellness

Job‑readiness coaching, community reintegration, and peer support for well‑being.
