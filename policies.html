Policies

Privacy

We collect minimal data when you contact or donate (name, email, message, and necessary transaction info) to respond, manage programs, and improve services. We do not sell data. We only share with processors or as required by law.

Storage: as required by tax/accounting law or until the purpose is fulfilled. Your rights: request access, correction, deletion; object/withdraw consent where applicable.

Terms of Use

Use the site responsibly. Content is informational and may change.

Donations & Receipts

Donations to Faithful Service for Veterans (EIN 85-1436299) may be tax‑deductible where permitted. Digital receipts are issued via email.

Non‑Discrimination & Political Activity

We do not discriminate on protected grounds, and we do not endorse candidates; lobbying limited as allowed by law.

Transparency

We maintain program and fund records and provide information upon valid request. See “Documents” for Articles and IRS letter.
